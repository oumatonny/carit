This evaluation system is designed to provide evaluate eligibility of student interested in assistant positions at CARIT. It is organized such that the grade standards for both undergraduates and graduate students are indicated. Through choosing student status (whether Graduate or Undergraduate) and reviewing of the given topics, a student can get their average mark and make sure they are eligible to continue to the application. The grading system ranges from A to F representing GPA from 4.0 to 0 respectively
